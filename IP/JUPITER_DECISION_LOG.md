# JUPITER™ Decision Engine — Decision Log

Purpose:
This log records all material recommendations generated by JUPITER™ Decision Engine
and the corresponding actions taken by the founder.

This log is designed to establish an auditable, time-ordered record of
decision quality, discipline, and process adherence.

---

## Logging Rules

- Every logged entry must be timestamped
- Recommendations must reflect JUPITER output, not hindsight
- Actions must reflect what was actually executed
- Deviations or overrides must be clearly noted

---

### Action Taken:

### Alignment:

### Notes:
(Optional reflections — no outcome bias)

## Audit Note

This log prioritizes process integrity over performance outcomes.
It is reviewed periodically to assess discipline, not short-term gains.
