# JUPITER™ Decision Engine — Override Log

Purpose:
This log records all instances where the founder overrides or deviates from
a recommendation generated by JUPITER™ Decision Engine.

The purpose of this log is to identify emotional interference, contextual
exceptions, and areas where system logic may require refinement.

---

## Logging Rules

- Every override must be logged immediately
- Rationalizations are not acceptable; reasons must be factual
- Outcomes are recorded later, not at the time of override
- Patterns matter more than individual events

## Audit Note

Overrides are expected but not encouraged.
This log exists to improve system trust and founder discipline over time.
