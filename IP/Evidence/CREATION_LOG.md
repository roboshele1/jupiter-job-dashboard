# JUPITER Decision Engine — Creation Log

Creator / Owner:
Edwin Asamoa Adoo

Project Name:
JUPITER™ Decision Engine

Description:
JUPITER Decision Engine is a proprietary, institutional-grade capital intelligence and decision-support system.
It is designed to ingest market data, apply governance and risk logic, adapt to changing economic regimes,
and support long-term, disciplined capital allocation decisions.

The system includes a desktop application, backend engines, decision logic, governance layers,
and adaptive learning components.

Date of Initial Concept:
October 19, 2025 — Saint John, New Brunswick, Canada

Status:
Actively developed and maintained as a private system.
